No single board is “the truth”: a preference board (LMArena) measures blind human votes, an intelligence index (Artificial Analysis) measures aggregate benchmarks, and code needs both “real engineering tasks” and “competitive algorithms.” It closes with animated charts reviewing how the leading models changed over ten years.

① 综合对话能力(LMArena Elo)① Overall conversation (LMArena Elo)

最广为引用的「综合」榜是 LMArena(前 LMSYS Chatbot Arena):用户对两个匿名模型的回答盲选,数百万次投票聚合成 Elo。它测的是人类对回答的整体偏好,而非某一项硬指标。 The most widely cited “overall” board is LMArena (formerly LMSYS Chatbot Arena): users blind-vote between two anonymous answers, and millions of votes aggregate into Elo. It measures overall human preference on answers, not any single hard metric.

注:Anthropic 新发布的 Claude Fable 5 在多个榜单登顶,但发布初期可用性受限(常回退到 Opus 4.8),因此「可日常使用」的综合榜首通常落在 Claude Opus 4.8 / GPT-5.5 Pro。 Note: Anthropic's newly released Claude Fable 5 tops several boards, but availability is limited at launch (it frequently falls back to Opus 4.8), so the “daily-usable” overall #1 is usually Claude Opus 4.8 / GPT-5.5 Pro.

② 综合智力与知识(Artificial Analysis)② Intelligence & knowledge (Artificial Analysis)

「综合知识/智力」最常用的复合指标是 Artificial Analysis Intelligence Index(v4.1),它把 10 个硬基准(含知识类的 GPQA Diamond、Humanity's Last Exam、知识与幻觉的 AA-Omniscience,以及 GDPval-AA 等 agentic 任务)加权成一个分数。 The most-used composite for “intelligence/knowledge” is the Artificial Analysis Intelligence Index (v4.1), weighting 10 hard benchmarks — including knowledge ones (GPQA Diamond, Humanity's Last Exam, the knowledge-and-hallucination AA-Omniscience) and agentic tasks like GDPval-AA — into a single score.

单看「知识」:Artificial Analysis 的知识与幻觉基准 AA-Omniscience 由 Claude Fable 5(40 分)领跑,较前任领头羊 Gemini 3.1 Pro Preview(33)高出 7 分,且主要来自更高的正确率而非更低的幻觉。这与「研究生级科学问答」GPQA Diamond 和超难综合知识考 Humanity's Last Exam 的趋势一致——头部依旧是 Anthropic / OpenAI / Google 三家轮流刷新。 On knowledge alone: Artificial Analysis's knowledge-and-hallucination benchmark AA-Omniscience is led by Claude Fable 5 (40), +7 over the previous leader Gemini 3.1 Pro Preview (33), driven mainly by higher accuracy rather than lower hallucination. That tracks with graduate-level science QA (GPQA Diamond) and the ultra-hard Humanity's Last Exam — the head is still Anthropic / OpenAI / Google trading record scores.

③ 代码能力(SWE-bench × LiveCodeBench)③ Code ability (SWE-bench × LiveCodeBench)

代码不能只看一个数,要拆成两种:真实软件工程(给定一个真实仓库的 issue,让 agent 改对代码并通过测试)看 SWE-bench Verified;竞赛/算法编程(防数据污染的新鲜题)看 LiveCodeBench。有趣的是:两边的冠军是不同阵营。 Code can't be a single number — split it in two: real software engineering (fix a real repo's issue so its tests pass) via SWE-bench Verified, and competitive/algorithmic programming (fresh, contamination-free problems) via LiveCodeBench. The twist: the two winners come from different camps.

这组对比是本笔记最有信息量的一张图:「会写算法」≠「会做工程」。Anthropic 的 Claude 在多步、要调工具、要读懂大仓库的真实工程任务上断层领先(SWE-bench 前四全是 Claude);而 DeepSeek、Qwen、Kimi 等开放权重模型在「干净的竞赛算法题」上反超闭源(LiveCodeBench 前六)。选型结论:做 编码 agent / 真实仓库改动 选 Claude 系;做 竞赛/算法/可自托管且省钱 选 DeepSeek / Qwen 系。 This pairing is the most informative chart here: “can do algorithms” ≠ “can do engineering.” Anthropic's Claude leads by a wide margin on multi-step, tool-using, large-repo real engineering (the SWE-bench top 4 are all Claude); while open-weights models like DeepSeek, Qwen and Kimi overtake the closed frontier on clean competitive problems (the LiveCodeBench top 6). Selection rule: for coding agents / real-repo changes pick the Claude family; for contests/algorithms/self-hosted & cheap pick DeepSeek / Qwen.

④ 十年演进(动图)④ Ten-year evolution (animated)

把镜头拉到十年尺度:从 2017 年的 Transformer 论文,到今天彼此咬合的前沿,「排名前列的模型」换了好几拨主角。下面第一张图是十年里程碑时间线(逐年点亮),第二张是「领跑者」更替的接力棒。 Zoom out to a decade: from the 2017 Transformer paper to today's interlocked frontier, the “leading models” changed protagonists several times. Below, the first chart is a ten-year milestone timeline (lighting up year by year), the second a leadership relay of who set the pace.

十年的两条主线很清楚:(1) 能力的来源从「更大的预训练规模」(2020 GPT-3)迁移到「对齐与产品化」(2022 ChatGPT),再到「测试时计算 / 推理」(2024–25 的 o 系列、R1);(2) 开源与闭源的差距持续缩小——2023 还是 GPT-4 一枝独秀,到 2026 开放权重(DeepSeek/Qwen)已在智力指数上进入前十、在竞赛代码上登顶。这意味着未来的「前十」会越来越像一张多极、按场景分工的榜,而不是单一霸主。 Two through-lines stand out over the decade: (1) the source of capability moved from “bigger pretraining scale” (2020 GPT-3) to “alignment & productization” (2022 ChatGPT) to “test-time compute / reasoning” (the o-series and R1 in 2024–25); (2) the open-vs-closed gap keeps narrowing — GPT-4 stood alone in 2023, but by 2026 open weights (DeepSeek/Qwen) reach the intelligence top-10 and top the competitive-code board. That implies the future “top ten” looks increasingly multipolar and task-specialized, rather than one dominant model.

怎么看榜 & 来源How to read these boards & sources

  • 没有「全场最佳」,只有「场景最佳」。对话偏好(LMArena)、综合智力(Artificial Analysis)、真实工程(SWE-bench)、竞赛算法(LiveCodeBench)四张榜的冠军并不相同。生产部署普遍采用「多模型路由」。There is no “best overall,” only “best for the task.” Preference (LMArena), intelligence (Artificial Analysis), real SE (SWE-bench), and competitive algorithms (LiveCodeBench) crown different winners. Production deployments increasingly use multi-model routing.
  • 注意「可用性」与「版本」。榜首常是限量/预览版本(如 Claude Fable 5),日常可用的最强可能是次席。Mind “availability” and “version.” The #1 is often a limited/preview variant (e.g. Claude Fable 5); the strongest daily-usable model may be the runner-up.
  • 警惕数据污染与刷分。静态基准(HumanEval、老版 SWE-bench)会被「针对性微调」刷高,因此本笔记优先采用防污染 / 滚动更新 / 人类盲选的榜单。Beware contamination and benchmark gaming. Static benchmarks (HumanEval, old SWE-bench) can be gamed by targeted fine-tuning, so this note prefers contamination-free / rolling / blind-human boards.
