Enhanced DeblurGAN:低光去模糊笔记Enhanced DeblurGAN: Low-light Deblurring Notes

这是我第一篇一作论文背后的工程笔记。问题很具体:夜间或低光环境下拍摄的照片,往往同时存在 低照度 和 运动模糊 两个退化。大多数方法只解决其中一个,串联起来又会互相放大噪声。 These are the engineering notes behind my first first-author paper. The problem is concrete: photos shot at night or in low light usually suffer from both low illumination and motion blur. Most methods tackle only one, and chaining two of them tends to amplify each other's noise.

核心想法The core idea

与其串联两个独立模型,不如把低光增强和运动去模糊 组合进一个 GAN,让生成器在一次前向里同时处理两种退化,用联合损失约束。这样能避免"先增强、噪声被放大、再去模糊、细节被抹平"的恶性链路。 Instead of chaining two separate models, combine low-light enhancement and motion deblurring into one GAN, so the generator handles both degradations in a single forward pass under a joint loss. That avoids the vicious chain of "enhance first → amplify noise → then deblur → wash out detail."

几个踩过的坑A few lessons learned

  • 损失权重很敏感:感知损失给太重,图会变"塑料感";给太轻,模糊去不干净。Loss weighting is sensitive: too much perceptual loss makes images look plastic; too little leaves residual blur.
  • 低光数据稀缺,合成数据和真实数据的分布差异要靠数据增强弥补。Low-light data is scarce; the gap between synthetic and real distributions has to be bridged with augmentation.
  • 判别器太强会让训练早期发散,加谱归一化稳很多。An over-powered discriminator diverges early in training; spectral normalization stabilized it a lot.
